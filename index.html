What is this?

Project Diagnonsense is a free symptom tracker for people navigating undiagnosed or early-stage inflammatory conditions — particularly where symptoms are episodic, bilateral, or hard to document at a clinical appointment.

It tracks joint temperatures against control sites, ring sizes, bilateral pain with type detail, headache patterns, sleep/stress/exertion triggers, weather and sun, medications, and photo flagging. Everything is designed to produce objective, timestamped data for a rheumatologist or specialist.

How your data works — and why it's yours

This app stores nothing on any server we control. When you press Save and sync, your entry is sent directly to a Google Sheet that you created and own.

The connection works through a small Google Apps Script you paste into your own Sheet during setup. The script runs entirely within your own Google account.

To export: open your Google Sheet at any time and download as CSV or Excel. Share with a doctor, or delete the sheet to delete everything.

Temperature protocol

Use an infrared non-contact thermometer (12:1 spot ratio, 0.1°C resolution). Always measure from the same distance, in the same room conditions.

Inner forearm — baseline for elbows + hands.

Calf — baseline for knees + hips.

Forehead — baseline for jaw readings.

Room temp — environmental context. Skin temp drifts with the room.

The differential (joint minus control) is what matters clinically — not the absolute reading.
