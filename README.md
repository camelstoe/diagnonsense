# Project Diagnonsense

A free, open-source symptom tracker for people navigating undiagnosed or early-stage inflammatory joint conditions — particularly where symptoms are difficult to document at a clinical appointment.

## Live app

[Open Project Diagnonsense](https://camelstoe.github.io/diagnonsense/)

## What it tracks

- Joint temperatures against two anatomical control sites, with automatic differentials
- Left and right sides logged separately for all paired joints
- Circumference measurements for all fingers, knees, and elbows
- Pain scores with type detail (burning, throbbing, stabbing etc.) per joint
- Headache location, type, and area
- Sun exposure with UV index and skin coverage detail
- Swelling and photo flagging by joint
- Medications per time slot
- Ice wanted / applied per joint

## Your data is yours

All entries go directly from your device to a Google Sheet you create and own. Nothing passes through any third-party server. No accounts, no tracking, no advertising. Delete your sheet at any time.

## How to use

1. Open the live app link above
2. Follow the **Setup tab** — takes about 5 minutes, requires a free Google account
3. Add to your phone home screen via Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android)
4. Paste the same Google Sheets URL on any other device to sync entries

## For developers

Fork the repository, make improvements, submit a pull request. The entire app is a single HTML file with no dependencies or build steps. Contributions welcome.
